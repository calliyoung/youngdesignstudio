About Me

A Professor Who
Builds Real Things

My name is Calli Osgood. I'm a Computer Information Technology professor with a passion for graphic design. I started OzDesign Studio because I noticed many small businesses in our community with websites that don't reflect the quality of what they do.

When you work with me, you work with me, not a team you'll never meet. I handle everything personally: design, development, branding, and support. Based right here in Owensboro, I care about this community and the businesses that make it thrive.

How It Works

My Simple 4-Step Process

1

Discovery Call

We start with a free conversation to talk through your business, your goals, and what you're hoping a website or brand can do for you.

2

Proposal & Plan

I put together a proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing. No hidden fees, no surprises, just a straightforward agreement we both feel good about.

3

Design & Build

I get to work and keep you in the loop with regular updates and check-ins. Your feedback shapes the outcome every step of the way.

4

Launch & Support

Your site goes live and I stay involved. For a small monthly fee, I handle updates, security, and any changes you need. No obligation for support of that does not fit your business model.
